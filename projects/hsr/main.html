About Honkai: Star Rail

Honkai: Star Rail is a turn-based strategy role-playing game developed by miHoYo. Set in a vibrant sci-fi universe, players take on the role of the protagonist who embarks on a journey aboard the Astral Express, a space train that travels across various worlds. The game features a rich narrative, a diverse cast of characters, and a blend of futuristic and fantastical elements. Players assemble a team of heroes, each with unique abilities and backgrounds, to engage in strategic battles against various enemies and uncover the mysteries of the universe. The game combines deep storytelling with immersive gameplay mechanics, appealing to fans of both sci-fi and RPG genres.
